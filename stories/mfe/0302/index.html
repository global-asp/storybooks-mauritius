Get dife!

Get dife!

Regardez, le feu !

Look, fire!

Avek dife, nou brile.

Avek dife, nou brile.

Le feu brûle.

Fire burns.

Avek dife, nou kwi.

Avek dife, nou kwi.

Le feu cuit.

Fire cooks.

Dife donn saler.

Dife donn saler.

Le feu donne de la chaleur.

Fire gives warmth.

Dife donn lalimier.

Dife donn lalimier.

Le feu donne de la lumière.

Fire gives light.

Get dife!

Get dife!

Regardez, le feu !

Look, fire!

Dife, enn mervey.

Dife, enn mervey.

Le feu est merveilleux.

Fire is wonderful.

Dife, li pwisan.

Dife, li pwisan.

Et il est puissant.

And it is powerful.

Written by: Deborah Namugosa, Beatrice Nabune, Alison Tukei, Rose Sabano
Illustrated by: Rob Owen
Translated by: Shameem Oozeerally & MIE French Students
Language: Mauritian Creole
Level: Level 1
Source: Fire from African Storybook
